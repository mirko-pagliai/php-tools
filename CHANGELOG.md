## 1.0 branch

* `unlink_recursive()` returns void.

* added `unlink_recursive()` and `safe_unlink_recursive()` functions.

* added `file_exists_or_fail()`, `is_dir_or_fail()`, `is_readable_or_fail()` and

### 1.0.4

* added `is_external_url()` global function;
